A hot, crispy churro with a mug of thick dark chocolate dip is what dreams are made of. Also known as “Spanish doughnuts”, churros are immensely popular in Europe and the United States. And the UAE has fallen in love with this deep-fried snack, too. Churros in Dubai come in endless variations to suit all tastes, but one thing remains constant…deliciousness!

Want to know where you can find the tastiest churros Dubai offers? At MyBayut, we’ve got you covered with a list of churros places in Dubai. Let’s begin the quest for good – no, GREAT – churros!

WHAT ARE CHURROS?

The origin of the churro is still unknown. According to some, the Spanish churro is a descendant of the Chinese youtiao pastry. According to folklore, Portuguese explorers brought youtiao to Iberia, evolving its distinctive star shape and sweetness.

As the authentic Spanish churros are thin, you must use a star-shaped funnel tip to get the right form. As a result, the ridges become crispier. The sprinkling of cinnamon sugar dust on top makes people swoon.

FAQS

WHAT DO CHURROS TASTE LIKE?

On the outside, churros are crispy, but on the inside, they’re soft. They have the flavour of cinnamon doughnuts but with crispy ridges. The basic churro is created with only salt, water and flour. However, there are numerous variants to this basic recipe.

WHERE CAN YOU GET CHURROS IN ABU DHABI?

From stuffed churros to delicious dips, Churros Café at Al Bahar Corniche offers some of the best churros in Abu Dhabi.
